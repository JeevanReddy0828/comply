Proving compliance is the hard part

The Act doesn't just ask whether your AI is safe — it asks you to demonstrate it, with documentation, logging, oversight and risk-management evidence per control. Today that means spreadsheets, consultants, and screenshots assembled by hand. It goes stale the moment your system changes.
